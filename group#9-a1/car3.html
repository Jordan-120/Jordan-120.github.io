Chevy Cobalt SS 2004-2011

The Cobalt SS was made between 2004-2011. From 05-07 they came supercharged. They came will many heavy perfromas parts, but retained a low cost around 40k US. This includes Recro seats, boost gauge and limited shift diffrential. They were so well made that the held the fastest time on the Nerdburg ring in the front wheel drive catorgry for three years. In 2008, they made many upgardes. The main upgrade was the switched from a superchager to a turbo. The engine was also swapped out from a LSJ, to an LNF. Other major upgrades include Brembo Brakes, F35 suspions, Launch control, No-lift Shift and a heads up display called an RDP.

SUMMARY: Best American made tuner because I own one. Many features that cost thousands as upgrades were stock. The engines and body were incredibly durable and many can still be seen on the road today. Mixed with lost cost and maintenance it was our clear choice.
